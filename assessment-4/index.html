The exciting Train Game
where you are the conductor

Taxe is a two player, turn-based Player versus Player (PvP) game in which players plan and route trains across Europe, competing to become the greatest train company of all time!

Cross Platform

The game has been tested and is working
on Windows, Linux, Mac OS X and Android tablets.

All the Trains you want

From the slow but environment-friendly
"green" train to the magnetic levitation train.

Let natural disasters strike!

These obstacles make the game unpredictable...
and more fun to play!

Competitive

Players acquire score from completing goals.
First to 200 wins!

Brought to you by Team EEP

TaxE has been developed over a lifetime of seven months between three development teams: FVS, ADB and now EEP. With the contributions of three development teams, you can be reassured that the game is reliable and fun to play. Three different perspectives have gone into the game to give you the best possible gameplay experience.
